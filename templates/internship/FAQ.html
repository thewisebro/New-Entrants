FAQs

Why is my resume empty?
Fill the details in Placement Online and fill out the details under 'Project Information' and 'Internship Information'.
Why do all the " s that I have filled up in various fields show up as # s in the resume?
This happened most probably because you copy-pasted the content from Microsoft Word document. Word uses a different " for formatting than the standard one. To solve this problem, after copy-pasting replace all the " s manually, that is type " s in through the keyboard. Generate the resume again. This should solve the problem.
Can I fill my B.Tech Project under Projects/Research?
Yes, you can fill your B.Tech Project under Project/Research.
Can you elaborate on what is meant by references, in the placement profile?
Faculty,Professionals or Guides to whom the company can contact so that they can get a feel for who you are. Select people to validate and reinforce what you have stated in your resume. A resume reference is some one who can verify your credentials and all or part of the information you have written in your resume.You have to know your references well and be sure that they can give a good feedback, to anybody asking questions about your past.
What will be the course code for B.Tech Dual Degree students?
Check the list for the course codes given on the Welcome Page.
I am having problem regarding the entry of percentage of my 10th, 12th? What is to be filled in C.G.P.A. for odd semester?
You have to fill the details in the Score for the percentage of 10th and 12th and the discipine as Not Applicable. At the end of odd sem too you get a C.G.P.A. so fill in that.

What to do if my image is in the right format(jpeg,gif..etc.) but still not uploading?
Your image must not be optimized, for that do the following: 1)Open the image in Adobe Photoshop. 2)Resize the image to required value. 3)Click on the option for save for web. 4)Open and check now.
