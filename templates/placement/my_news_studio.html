MyNewsStudio

Use MyNewsStudio to create your personal news channel by customizing news feeds from a huge selection of topics and sources!
The main features of MyNewsStudio are:
  1. News Aggregation: The application is primarily a news aggregator which allows the student to custom create his/her personalized news feed from over 200 major news related websites, newspapers, magazines and blogs. The clean design of the application makes reading fast and efficient.
  2. NewsGraphsTM: NewsGraphsTM are our trademarked info-graphics used to present complex news topics in a visually rich format; which helps in quick review of the information and data.
  3. Shared Reading List: Using this feature, an administrator is able to share his/her reading list with all the students.
  4. Glossary of terms: We have a glossary of major economic terms; seamlessly integrated in the application itself.
How to register?
A person with iitr.ernet.in can directly access all the premium services. You only need to register using your IITR webmail id and subsequent to your registration, a verification mail is sent on the registered id. Go to My News Studio
