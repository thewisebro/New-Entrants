Frequently Asked Questions

Can I apply for both the profiles (Designer and Developer)?

Yes. Surely, you can.

Do I need to know coding beforehand?

No. You don’t need to. It’s just a bonus point.

I didn’t get a call but my name was there on the list. What should I do ?

Stay tuned. You will get a call. If you still don’t, contact us to sort it out.

How many people will you select?

Well, it depends and generally varies from 10 to 20.

I don't know how to use any design software. How do I submit my assignment?

You can submit hand drawn sketches at IMG lab anytime before the deadline.

I am a second year student. Can I apply?

Yes you can, but we are recruting only designers / frontend developers from second year. You can mail your work to recruitment.img@gmail.com before 11:59 pm, 14th Jan, 2016.

How do I prepare for the interview?

There are no prerequisites. Just drop by for some chit-chat.
