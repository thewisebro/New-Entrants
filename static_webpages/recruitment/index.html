FAQ

What are the perks of being an IMGian?
You get to learn and work with new and latest technologies, frameworks and programming, front-end and server-side languages like HTML/CSS, Javascript, Python, PHP, Ajax, Django, Yii, Git, SASS, Jade, Angular JS, Grunt, Yeoman, Photoshop, etc. We also focus on Android Development. We have an abundance of seniors who are proficient in all these languages and technologies. You can consult anytime them in case of doubts. You get to work on the official Intranet Portal of IITR and work on cool apps like Jukebox, Connect-e-Dil, Notice Board, etc. The lab is open 24 hours and 7 days a week.You can even sleep here in case you're working late.
What resources will I have access to, once I join IMG?

    How does joining IMG help me with my career?
    Over the years, IMGians have been recruited by the best companies in the world. Google, Microsoft, Facebook, Direct-I, Goldman Sachs...you name it and we have that in our list. IMG not only provides you with an experience of software/web application development, under the guidance of like-minded seniors, but also provides you with an overview of work culture of these companies, that you will experience during subsequent years in your life. It also is an oppurtunity for non-CS students to develop their coding skills and more importantly to develop the passion required for it.
    Is IMG a very formal group, where I'll just be burdened with deadlines?
    No! Hell No! We organise dance parties, late night movies, surprise lab chapos and the best farewell party in IITR. That's right! It takes a month to plan our annual farewell party. When it comes to trips, we don't just go rafting in Rishikesh, bathing in Haridwar or to a cave in Dehradhun. We explore the most beautiful hill stations in North India. We have the fastest internet in the campus and only the best resources at our disposal. IMG is not a group. It is a family!
